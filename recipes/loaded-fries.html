Loaded Fries Recipe

Description

Loaded bacon fries are a divine indulgence, a tantalizing symphony of flavors and textures. It all starts with a foundation of golden, crispy fries, perfectly cooked to a satisfying crunch. Then comes the lavish layering of melted cheddar or gooey cheese sauce, generously draping each fry in a velvety embrace.

But the true star of this decadent dish is the bacon—crisped to perfection, its savory aroma and smoky essence infuse every bite. The bacon, diced or crumbled, is generously scattered atop the bed of fries, adding a tantalizing contrast of saltiness and richness that elevates the entire ensemble.

To amplify the sensory experience, a medley of toppings often joins the affair: perhaps some finely chopped green onions for a fresh, zesty kick, a dollop of tangy sour cream, or even a sprinkle of jalapeños for those seeking an extra fiery punch.

The resulting creation is a harmony of flavors—salty, smoky, creamy, and tangy—each element playing its part in a mouthwatering crescendo that leaves taste buds dancing with delight. Loaded bacon fries are not just a dish; they are an irresistible celebration of indulgence and culinary bliss.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Green onions (scallions)
  • Sour cream
  • Jalapeños
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Guacamole
  • Cilantro
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder

Preparation & steps:

  1. Prepare the Fries: Slice the potatoes into fries, season them with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder, then bake or fry until they're crispy and golden.

  2. Cook the Bacon: While the fries are cooking, crisp up the bacon in a skillet until it's nice and crunchy. Once done, chop or crumble it into small pieces.

  3. Melt the Cheese: Prepare a cheese sauce or melt shredded cheese to pour over the cooked fries.

  4. Assemble the Dish: Place the crispy fries on a serving dish. Pour the melted cheese over the fries and sprinkle the crispy bacon on top.

  5. Add Toppings: Garnish your loaded fries with chopped green onions (scallions), sliced jalapeños for a kick of heat, diced tomatoes for freshness, guacamole for creaminess, chopped cilantro for extra flavor, and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes if you desire more heat.

  6. Serve: Present your loaded bacon fries while they're still hot and enjoy the delicious combination of flavors and textures!
